Our Story

Speedy Bite was founded in 2023 with one goal in mind - to deliver fresh, delicious meals from local restaurants as quickly as possible. We started as a small operation with just a few drivers, but demand grew quickly based on our commitment to speedy service.

Now Speedy Bite operates in cities across the country, with a large fleet of drivers and expanded restaurant partnerships. However, our commitment to quick delivery times and customer satisfaction remains unchanged. We pride ourselves on getting your order picked up and delivered within 30 minutes or less.

At Speedy Bite, we know that when you're hungry, every minute counts. Let us zoom to your favorite restaurants and deliver your meal fast, so you can focus on enjoying your food. With our commitment to speed, service and satisfaction, Speedy Bite has become the go-to delivery option for those craving a fresh, hot meal in a flash!

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the fastest and most reliable on-demand food delivery service, satisfying customers' cravings with exceptional speed and service.
